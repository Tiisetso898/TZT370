Company Background

Tribal Zone Trading is a South African multidisciplinary civil and construction contractor, combining extensive experience with professional, theoretical, and technical expertise to deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards.

Tribal Zone Trading was founded in 2004 and has since operated successfully. The company continues to grow and develop as a sustainable, black-owned enterprise.

We are a 100% Black owned enterprise with Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) grading of 4CE-PE and 4GB-PE, and are registered with NHBRC, SARS and CSD.

Our Vision

To become a nationwide well known multi-disciplinary civil, construction consulting company and to mentor upcoming entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to render excellent services to the clients and the community at large. To empower Tribal Zone Trading employees and offer job opportunities to the community at large.

Why Choose Tribal Zone Trading?

Tribal Zone Trading has a proven track record of delivering quality civil infrastructure projects on time and within budget. The company demonstrates strong capability in large-diameter pipelines, sewer networks, and water reticulation systems, supported by an experienced multidisciplinary team with both technical and practical expertise.

Tribal Zone Trading is committed to community empowerment, skills development, and black economic empowerment, and is fully compliant and registered with all relevant industry and regulatory bodies.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, Tribal Zone Trading 370 CC has established itself as a reliable and competent player in South Africa's civil engineering and construction sector. With nearly two decades of experience, we have successfully delivered numerous infrastructure projects that have contributed to community development and economic growth.

As a 100% Black-owned enterprise, we are proud of our role in transformation and empowerment within the construction industry. Our CIDB grading of 4CEPE and 4GBPE demonstrates our capability to handle projects of significant size and complexity.
